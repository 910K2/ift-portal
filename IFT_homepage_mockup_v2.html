Who We Are
We exist because fleets cannot afford
the luxury of guesswork.

InfoTrack was built by people who believed that location data alone was never enough. Every vehicle carries risk, cargo, lives. For over two decades, we have engineered solutions that don't just track — they understand, alert, and protect. Today we are rebuilding for the next era: MDVR, sensor fusion, and a zero-fault engineering culture that holds every signal accountable from hardware to dashboard.

01
Zero-Fault Engineering
Every fault is traced to root cause. We have built a dedicated RCA function with end-to-end signal visibility — hardware through backend to frontend. "Reset it" is not in our vocabulary.
02
Real Integration Depth
MDVR, ADAS, DMS, fuel sensors, CAN bus, custom I/O — correlated on a single timeline. We commit only to what we can deliver, and we deliver what we commit.
03
Two Decades of Trust
ISO 9001:2015 certified. Backed by FIG Inc., listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime Market. Serving Toyota, Shell, Ford, Ericsson and dozens of India's most demanding fleet operators.
